What is Cat Town?

Welcome to Cat Town, a relaxing game where you can collect cats and combine fluffy creatures to earn more cryptocurrency (ETH) and special rewards ($KIBBLE). It's all about growing your digital kitty collection and earning cool bonuses along the way!

Getting Started in Cat Town
  • To participate in activities like Lucky Fish Tickets, you'll need at least 100 cats.
    • Acquiring Cats:
    • Cats are bought using ETH (Ethereum).
    • Currently, 0.001 ETH can get you approximately 15 cats. Note that prices may change with market conditions.
    Generating Floofs:
    • Once you own cats, each cat generates 1 floof per second.
    • Floofs can be combined to create more cats, increasing your yield over time.
    • Alternatively, you can sell floofs to realize your ETH gains.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What wallet is recommended?

    Consider using Coinbase Wallet or Smart Wallet (for free rod), as they are known for their low gas fees, which can be advantageous for your transactions.

    2. How much is 1 cat?

    The price of cats fluctuates based on the current Ethereum (ETH) price. Currently, 1 cat is priced at approximately 15PHP.

    3. What is $KIBBLE?

    In addition to ETH, KIBBLE serves as the primary currency in Cat Town. It is utilized for purchasing cosmetic items for your cat, acquiring adventure-boosting items, for fishing, and funding player rewards within the game environment.

    4. What is Fishing?

    Fishing in Cat Town offers numerous items that can be sold for KIBBLE currency. Additionally, participants can compete on the leaderboard; those catching the heaviest fish have the opportunity to earn substantial rewards in KIBBLE.

    5. How to start fishing?

    To begin fishing, you'll need a fishing rod, KIBBLE worth $0.2 USD, and ETH valued at $0.05 USD per fish. For 10 fish, you'll require KIBBLE worth $2 USD and ETH valued at $0.5 USD. Additionally, please ensure you have extra ETH to cover transaction gas fees.

    6. How much is the fishing rod?

    During the fishing week in Cat Town, you have the option to purchase a fishing rod for 25$ of KIBBLE. Alternatively, Smart Wallet users can claim a free rod. Keep in mind also that fishing rod is lifetime investment

    7. How to create smart wallet account?

    To get started, visit cat.town in your web browser, connect your Coinbase Wallet, and follow the prompts to create a Smart Wallet account. Once completed, you'll have successfully set up your Smart Wallet account!

    8. How long will it take to ROI?

    You can expect to achieve your Return on Investment (ROI) in approximately 3.3 months. Keep in mind that various factors can influence your target ROI.

    9. Where can I buy KIBBLES and ETH?

    On Telegram, we host a channel where merchants are verified by our administrators for a 5% transaction fee

    10. How can I take profit?

    You can profit by selling floofs for ETH or combining cats to earn KIBBLE. Please note that only 100 cat owners are eligible for the KIBBLE reward system.

    11. How can I earn KIBBLES?

    You can earn KIBBLE by acquiring 3 tickets. Each time you combine or purchase cats, you earn tickets. Tickets can be claimed once per day, with claiming resetting at 8 AM PHT.
